You are playing the sequel, simply titled *Clock Tower* for the PS1 (also known as *Clock Tower 2* in some regions). This is a different game with different characters (Jennifer is no longer the protagonist) and a different structure.

For the PS1 *Clock Tower*, "getting all characters to survive" is a more complex goal, as the game features multiple protagonists and branching paths. The best ending is achieved by successfully completing the game as **Jennifer** and getting the **"A" ending**.

Based on a detailed walkthrough for the game , here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best possible outcome where the maximum number of characters survive.

This guide follows the "Jennifer 100%" route, which aims for the A ending and the survival of 7 characters .

**1. Prologue - Samuel Barton (Police Station)**
Your first task is to thoroughly investigate the police station as Samuel.
- **Initial Investigation:** Examine the Scissors, the Bench, and the Exit Door.
- **Main Office:** Investigate the Statue. Talk to Beth twice. Investigate the Mask and then the Computer next to it. Talk to the Blond Man twice, then leave through the left door.
- **Down the Hall:** Go Right. Talk to Harris twice. Take the elevator to floor 1 for a long interview, then take the elevator to floor 2 after.
- **Back to the Office:** Return to the main office and talk to Harris again. Investigate the Statue and when prompted, choose **"YES, GIVE TO RICK"** .
- **Final Step:** Enter the Therapy room next to Harris, talk to Kay and Edward. The prologue will end as the screen loads.

**2. Jennifer's Date**
- Follow the map: Go to the **University Research Building** and talk to Helen.
- Then, go to the **International Hotel** and talk to Kay.
- Finally, go to the **University Staff Housing** for the "Cheesy Date" scene.

**3. Scenario 1 (University)**
- Walk right to the university. The Scissorman chase will begin.
- Enter the first door. **Hide in the Box.** After he leaves, check the bookshelf near the box to find the **Oil Can**.
- Run down the hallway to the door right of the elevator. Investigate the desk inside to find the **Ladder Key**.
- Leave the room, go to the Stairwell, and use the Ladder Key at the top of the staircase to climb down and escape for now.

**4. Intermission 1**
- Go to the University Research Building and talk to Helen.
- Then, go to the Oslo Weekly Newspaper office and talk to Nolan. When he makes his proposal, you **CHOOSE YES** .

**5. Scenario 2 (Rick's House)**
- After the old man dies, go through the right door and upstairs. Enter the door next to the stairs and grab the **Blanket**.
- Exit and re-enter the room. This time, investigate the green closet to find the **Demon Idol**.
- Go back downstairs to the living room.
- Enter the room on the far left of the living room, then the room on the far right of that hallway. Grab the **Soap** on the shelf by the entrance.
- Return to the living room. Enter the room on the right by the couches. **Crucially, investigate the mask on the wall in the kitchen** to reveal the location of the Barrows mansion. The mask will come to life. Use the vase on the table to throw at the mask and defeat it.
- Leave the room and "prepare" the soap (select it in your inventory). Go outside and use the soap on the dog to escape unharmed.

**6. Intermission 2**
- Return to the Oslo Newspaper and chat with Nolan.
- Go to the Police Station and chat with everyone.
- Go to the Hotel and chat with everyone.
- Go to the University and chat with everyone .
